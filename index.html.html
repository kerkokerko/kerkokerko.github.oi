About Us

Profile: DS Educational Foundation Inc.

Background And Rationale:

Established in December 2024, the DS Educational Foundation was born out of a pressing need to address the significant gaps in education, health, youth development and agriculture development in Liberia.
According to recent statistics, only 35% of children in rural Liberia have access to quality primary education, while secondary school enrollment in Nimba County stands at a mere 28%.

The healthcare sector faces similar strugles, with over 40% of the population lacking access to basic health services. Agriculture, which forms the backbone of Liberia's economy, is hindered by outdated methods, with less than 20% of farmers utilizing modern techniques.

Orphanages in Liberia face additional challenges. Nimba County alone has over 50 recognized orphanages, while Liberia hosts more than 200 orphanages, both recognized and unrecognized. Many of these institutions lacked proper funding, adquate facilities, and trained personel, with over 70% strugling to meet basic needs.
The DS Educational Foundation steps in to bridge these gaps, starting with Nimba County.Our initiatives focus on empowering underprivileged youth through technical and vocational train, supporting orphanages with educational resources, and collaboration with local farmers to modernize agricultural practices. By leveraging community-driven approaches, we aim to expand our impact across Liberia and eventually the West African region.


Mission Statement:

To empower underpriviledged children and youth throught education, technical training, and diversified development initiatives, forstering, cooperation, dialogue, and collaboration to create lasting change in Liberia and the West African region.

Vision Statement:

To create a society where every child and youth has access to quality education, health services, and substainable development opportunities, driving growth and prosperity across Liberia and West Africa.

Core Values:

Empathy: We deeply understand the challenges faced by vulnerable communities and work tiredlessly to alleviate them.
Collaboration: Partnering with local and international stakeholders toachieve our goals.

Services


Our Approach:

Education:The DS Educational Foundation is committed to transforming education by providing scholarships to underprivileged stundents; ensuring financial barriers do not hinder access to learning. Our initiatives include distribution of learning materials like textbooks, digital tools and other resources to enhance the quality of education in schools. Additionally, we prioritize teacher training programs, equipping educators with modern teaching methodologies to inspire and empower students.
By fostering an inclusive and well-supported learning environment. We aim to increase enrollment rates, reduce dropout levels, and improve academic performance across Nimba County and Liberia as a whole.

Healthcare: Our healthcare initiatives address the critical gaps in medical service delivery by supporting local medical facilities with essential resources such as medical supplies, equipment, and infrastructure development.

The DS Educational Foundation also invests in training healthcare workers, enabling them to provide better and more efficient care to underserved communities.
Our focus extends to health education, promoting preventive measures, and raising awareness about hygiene, nutrition, and disease prevention. Through these efforts, we aim to reduce disparities, improve maternal and child health, and build resilient communities in Nimba Countyand beyond.

Agriculture: Recognizing the vital role agriculture plays in Liberia's economy, the DS Educational Foundation works to modernize farming practices in rural areas.
We provide farmers with access to high-level seeds, modern tools, and training in substainable agricultural techniques.
By introducing innovations such as irrigation systems and soil testing, we help improve crop yields and food security.

Additionally, we collaborate with local cooperatives to connect farmers to markets, ensuring fair prices for their produce. These efforts not only enhance livelihoods but also contribute to economic growth and the reduction of poverty in Nimba County.

Ophanage Support: The DS Educational Foundation is dedicated to uplift orphanages by providing essential resources such as food, clothing, educational supplies, and improved living conditions.

Beyond material support, we offer training programs for caregivers to ensure they can provide nurturing and effective care to children.

Our initiative also include creative recreational and develop mental activities that promote mental activities that promote mental and emotional well-being, by addressing the pressing needs of orphanages, we aim to create a safer and supportive environment for vulnerable children, empowering them to thrive and become productive members of society.

In conclusion: The DS Educational Foundation believes in the transformative power of collective action.
Together, we can create a brighter future for Liberia, starting with Nimba County, and inspire change across the West African region. Your support can make this Vision a reality.
